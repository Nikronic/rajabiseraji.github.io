A Survey on Indoor Localization Methods

Sep 2016 - Currently

A Novel Way for Device Based Indoor Localization using Acoustic Waves in a WiFi Network
Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic)– A.U.T
Supervisor: Dr. Mehdi Sedighi
In-Progress, To be submitted to WINSYS 2019 and the next UbiComp
Sep 2016 - Currently

This is part of my thesis project and I've been working on it for the past year and a half. My main task in this project is to implement different Indoor mapping and Tracking technologies in the literature, both on dedicated and general purpose hardware. I've come up with a new way that combines acoustic mapping, Wi-Fi fingerprinting and Inetrial Measurement Sensors to acquire a 2D map of an indoor environment and actively track the connected users in it. This is a hot topic in networking and has quite a large demand in the market. We've implemented our method and have successfully tested it on five different devices, we're now looking into developing a testing framework for indoor localization methods and are extending Evarilos and EvAAL projects.
